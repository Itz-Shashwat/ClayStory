Our Story

A Tiny Studio,
Big Dreams

ClayStory started at a small desk with a pack of clay and a lot of imagination. Every piece you see here is hand-shaped, painted, and finished with care — no moulds, no machines, just hands and heart.

We believe the best gifts are the ones made with love. That's why every order is treated as a personal creation — whether it's a miniature of your pet, a tray for your jewellery, or a keychain that makes someone smile.
